## Honest assessment of the cohort

**Why this list is real:**
- 5 companies (Lannis, Facturitas, Genio Contable, Arqueo, Cuonti) are literally building products on top of AFIP+LLM TODAY. They have the exact pain Vultur solves and they have funding to pay.
- 10 more are 1 product update away from needing Vultur.
- The Platform-tier cohort (Pomelo, Tapi, Tiendanube, Increase, Wise CX) is the most lucrative — $8k-30k+ ACV potential, multi-tenant override fees compound.

**Why this list could shrink:**
- Some of the Hot-5 are pre-revenue or thin on capital. Lannis is actively spending on legal defense vs Consejo. Free tier might be required to land them.
- Some of the warm-15 might just build it themselves rather than buy. Especially the engineering-heavy teams (The Flock, Lenk).
- Pomelo and Tapi are big enough that buying-vs-building decisions go through committees. 6+ months sales cycle for either.

**Realistic first-90-days target:**
- Land 2-3 paying Hot customers (Lannis, Facturitas, Genio Contable best bets) — $300-1500/mo each = $6k-30k ARR.
- Land 1 Platform pilot (Tiendanube app ecosystem partner or Wise CX via Endeavor Bearzi intro) = potential $50k+ ARR if it converts.
- Total realistic Y1: $50-100k ARR. Aligns with SOM Y1 estimate.
