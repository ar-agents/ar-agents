# Sociedad Automatizada (Anteproyecto de Ley General de Sociedades)

The figure exists in a draft bill, not law yet: signed 28-may-2026, sent to the Senate 1-jun-2026, takes effect 180 days after publication if sanctioned. Say so when asked. Do not claim a company can already incorporate under art. 14.

Verified rules against the bill text:

- **Art. 14 (Automatización).** A company of any type (SRL, SA, SAS) that pursues its purpose through autonomous algorithmic systems or AI agents, without employees in a dependency relationship for ordinary operation, is a Sociedad Automatizada. The declaration must be in the estatuto. The denomination must include the word "Automatizada". It answers with its own assets for damage caused by its AI systems. It is NOT "zero humans".
- **Art. 88.** Administration is held by one or more humans or legal persons. There is always an administrator on record. If a legal person administers, art. 92 makes it name one human.
- **Art. 101.** Administrators are liable for fault or intent (obligation of means, not objective). It carries the business-judgment rule: an administrator who acts in good faith, informed, "con arreglo a un procedimiento de decisión adecuado", is not liable for the business decisions' outcome.
- **Art. 102 (Sistemas de IA en la Gestión).** Using AI for operations or decisions does NOT remove the administrators' liability and does NOT excuse the non-delegable duty to configure and supervise the system and its results. This is why the irreversible steps in this flow require human approval: it is the supervision the law demands.
- **Art. 263 (DAO).** Digital records substitute physical ones as long as they are publicly verifiable, reproducible and reconstruct the patrimonial state. This is the legal basis for the signed audit log.

How this maps to the flow: incorporation and any payment or filing are gated on human approval (the administrator), not because eve makes you, but because art. 102 does. Every action lands in the signed audit log so art. 101/102 can be proven later.
